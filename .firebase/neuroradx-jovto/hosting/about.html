About NeuroRadX

Empowering Neuroradiological Expertise

NeuroRadX is more than just a question bank; it is a comprehensive ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between clinical practice and academic excellence in neuroradiology.

What is NeuroRadX?

NeuroRadX provides a specialized learning environment for medical professionals focusing on neuroradiology. Our platform integrates advanced technology with expert medical knowledge to provide an unparalleled educational experience.

Key Features

Extensive Question Bank

  • Over 5,000 board-style questions.
  • Coverage of Head, Spine, Neck, and General Neuroradiology.

Personalized Study Modes

Tutor Mode: Immediate feedback and detailed explanations.

Exam Mode: Simulated test environments with timers.

Flashcards: Rapid-fire spaced repetition.

Detailed Progress Tracking

  • Visual analytics of performance.
  • Identification of weak areas.
  • Historical score comparisons.

Organizational Tools

Bookmarks: Save challenging questions for later.

My Notes: Personal annotations for every question.

Who is it for?

Designed for every stage of medical training and practice:

Medical Students:

Essential for neuroanatomy and clinical basics.

Residents & Fellows:

The perfect companion for board preparation.

Radiologists & Clinicians:

Continuous learning and clinical updates.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for neuroradiological education through innovation, accessibility, and expert validation.
